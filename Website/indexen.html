Statistical Learning Laboratory

The Statistical Learning Laboratory (SaLLy) is a hub for statistical and data science research, training, and consultancy. It brings together the research group of Professor Paulo Canas Rodrigues, including some of the main collaborators and students.

Our main directions

Research

The research at SaLLy focuses on the development and application of statistical learning methodologies in a wide range of topics in statistics and data science, and in all areas of application.

Read more

Training

SaLLy organizes statistical and data science courses at the beginner, intermediate and advanced levels and can be tailored to students and professionals in the private and public sectors.

Read more

Consultancy

SaLLy provides consultancy in statistics and data science projects for students, professionals, and companies in the private and public sectors, aiming at transforming data into information.

SaLLy - Evento
28 AUG
AIMM 2024 Campinas, São Paulo, Brazil

    Join us for a three-day intensive training organized by the Statistical Leaning Laboratory (SaLLy; www.SaLLy.ufba.br) and the State University of Campinas (UNICAMP), sponsored by the Applied Malaria Modelling Network (AMMnet; https://ammnet.org/), aiming to equip participants with the necessary skills and knowledge to employ artificial intelligence (AI) techniques, including machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL), in modeling malaria (and infectious diseases in general) spread and intervention strategies in Brazil and Latin America. This training aligns with AMMnet’s mission, leveraging advanced ML and DL methodologies to enhance disease surveillance, prediction, and control efforts, ultimately contributing to reducing malaria transmission and burden in the region.

2nd SaLLy Day Salvador, Bahia, Brazil

    SaLLy Day is an annual event designed to showcase the Statistical Learning Laboratory (SaLLy) and its principal activities, research areas, and projects. This engaging event offers students, researchers, and industry professionals a chance to learn about the cutting-edge research and innovative projects underway at SaLLy. Attendees can explore various presentations, demonstrations, and interactive sessions that highlight the lab's accomplishments and expertise in areas like data science, machine learning, and statistical analysis. The event also fosters networking opportunities, enabling participants to connect with laboratory members and other professionals, fostering future collaborations and knowledge exchange within the field.
