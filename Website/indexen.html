Statistical Learning Laboratory

The Statistical Learning Laboratory (SaLLy) is a hub for statistical and data science research, training, and consultancy. It brings together the research group of Professor Paulo Canas Rodrigues, including some of the main collaborators and students.

Our main directions

Research

The research at SaLLy focuses on the development and application of statistical learning methodologies in a wide range of topics in statistics and data science, and in all areas of application.

Read more

Training

SaLLy organizes statistical and data science courses at the beginner, intermediate and advanced levels and can be tailored to students and professionals in the private and public sectors.

Read more

Consultancy

SaLLy provides consultancy in statistics and data science projects for students, professionals, and companies in the private and public sectors, aiming at transforming data into information.
