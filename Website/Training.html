Training

The Statistical Learning Laboratory (SaLLy) organizes introductory, intermediate, and advanced statistical and data science courses. These courses are intended for professionals in the public and private sectors and BSc, MSc, and PhD students. These courses will be defined as online or in-person, and the participants and companies might be charged a registration fee. The fees vary from course to course, and some courses might be free of charge.


Some members of our team have experience in giving courses in statistics and data science to companies. The content and duration of these courses will be tailored to fulfill the requirements and interests of the required customer. Statistical software such as R, Python, and SPSS can be used for computational applications, depending on the customer’s interest.
